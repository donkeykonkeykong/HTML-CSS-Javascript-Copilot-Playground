Cheesy Egg and Lettuce Breakfast Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of French bread
  • 1 cup lettuce, washed and torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or your favorite type)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Prepare the Eggs:

  • Crack the eggs into a bowl, add milk, and whisk until well combined.
  • Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and melt 1 tablespoon of butter.
  • Pour the egg mixture into the skillet, and let it cook without stirring until the edges start to set.
  • Gently stir the eggs occasionally until they are fully cooked but still moist. Remove from heat.

Toast the French Bread:

  • While the eggs are cooking, toast the slices of French bread in a toaster or on a griddle until they are golden brown.

Assemble the Sandwich:

  • Place a slice of toasted French bread on a plate.
  • Spoon half of the scrambled eggs onto the bread.
  • Add a layer of torn lettuce on top of the eggs.
  • Sprinkle half of the shredded cheese over the lettuce.
  • Top with the second slice of toasted French bread.

Melt the Cheese:

  • Return the assembled sandwich to the skillet over medium-low heat.
  • Add the remaining tablespoon of butter to the skillet.
  • Carefully flip the sandwich after a minute to allow both sides to be buttered and the cheese to melt. Cook until the bread is golden brown.

Serve and Enjoy:

  • Once the cheese is melted and the bread is toasted to your liking, remove the sandwich from the skillet.
  • Allow it to cool for a moment before slicing in half.
  • Serve your delicious Cheesy Egg and Lettuce Breakfast Sandwich immediately.
